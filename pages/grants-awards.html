Grants & Awards

Institutional recognition and funded projects that validate our work in energy technology and power electronics.

🏆 Award

Winners of the IDEA Acelera 2024 Competition

Nogara Energy was awarded first place in the IDEA Acelera 2024 competition, organised by Fundación Aragón Emprende — one of the most prestigious entrepreneurship awards in the Aragonese ecosystem.

The recognition highlights our project transforming power electronics and energy flexibility from Aragón to a global scale. The initiative received support from Fundación Rafael del Pino and Aragón Exterior (AREX), and mentorship through the matriX programme at CEEIARAGON.

Organised by: Fundación Aragón Emprende · Instituto Aragonés de Fomento (IAF)

Funded Grant

Hydrogen Generation through Photovoltaic Energy in Combination with PLC Technology

Development of a scalable green hydrogen generation system for vehicles powered by photovoltaic energy, leveraging microinverters equipped with Power Line Communication (PLC) technology for intelligent energy coordination.

Funded by: Instituto Aragonés de Fomento (IAF) · Gobierno de Aragón
