Power Line Communication (NB-PLC)

Custom hardware and firmware for narrowband power line communication over existing electrical wiring — our core expertise.

We design end-to-end NB-PLC solutions using PRIME and G3 standards. From modem hardware to full communication stacks, we deliver products validated with DSOs, metering manufacturers and energy management integrators. Our proprietary nodes (Metrology Node, Linux Node, PLC Nodes) give us full control from silicon to application layer.

  • ✓ Custom NB-PLC modem hardware (PRIME / G3)
  • ✓ Firmware and communication stack development
  • ✓ Indoor PLC channel characterisation and analysis
  • ✓ Lab and field validation with real grid operators
  • ✓ Integration with DSO control and DLMS/COSEM
  • ✓ Technical publications and standards contribution
