We research, design and validate advanced NB-PLC solutions for energy flexibility — from concept to field deployment.

What we do

End-to-end NB-PLC research and development

We collaborate with DSOs, equipment manufacturers and international PLC standards alliances to design, implement and validate indoor NB-PLC solutions that integrate EVs, manageable loads and solar PV under a single communication channel.

  • ✓ Indoor NB-PLC architecture design (PRIME / G3)
  • ✓ Firmware and communication stack development
  • ✓ Lab and field validation with real operators
  • ✓ Technical publications and standards contribution
  • ✓ Custom hardware prototyping and testing

Our hardware

Proprietary NB-PLC nodes

We design and build our own hardware platforms — Metrology Node, Linux Node and PLC Nodes — enabling full control of the communication stack from silicon to application layer.

All nodes are designed for indoor deployment over existing electrical wiring, validated against PRIME 1.3 and 1.4 standards with multiple manufacturers.
