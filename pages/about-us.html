Who we are

Nogara Energy

A young company with deep roots in electronic engineering and a clear goal: to industrialise energy flexibility through indoor NB-PLC.

Our story

From the lab to the live grid

We were born from years of experience in advanced electronic engineering, with a team that has worked on PLC communication projects, channel characterisation and smart grid management.

Based in Zaragoza, Aragon, we built our technology on the electrical wiring that already exists in millions of homes: no Wi-Fi, no new gateways, no civil works. Just the existing infrastructure, put to work for the grid of the future.

Our vision is #ModernGrids: resilient distribution networks where every home is an active energy flexibility node, with EVs, loads and solar PV under coordinated DSO control.

Our technical approach

We industrialise indoor NB-PLC

Our bet is clear: indoor NB-PLC based on PRIME and G3 over existing electrical wiring. It is the only technology that provides DSO access without requiring IP connectivity from the home.

  • ✓ PRIME and G3 standards supported
  • ✓ Compatible with existing smart meters
  • ✓ Scalable from one home to thousands of nodes
  • ✓ Validated with PLC alliances and grid operators
